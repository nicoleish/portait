Who are we?

What started as a quick and easy way to eat sweets, Nickey started baking cupcakes whenever she was stressed and used baking as procrastination. At the end, she would have a sweet treat that would help her get through her undergraduate years at NYU.

What she didn't know was how much people liked them! Her buisness started small by handing out free cupcakes to those who came to her dorm room. Later she would bring them to her classes and be told,

"you should totally sell these!"
