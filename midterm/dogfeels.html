Can Dogs Understand Humans?🐶🚶‍

answer: hell f-ing yeah!

“Absolutely,” says Dr. JP McCue, a board-certified veterinary neurologist at NYC's Animal Medical Center. Dogs process sensation and emotion much like we do.

Studies have shown they’re capable of feeling:

  • Optimism
  • Anxiety
    • their brains respond to dog anxiety medication like Prozac. There is also evidence that supports that dogs who experience traumatic events experience symptoms of PTSD, just like humans.
  • Happiness
  • Fear
  • Depression
  • Jealousy
    • They get jealous when another dog gets a bigger reward for the same behavior

They also experience pain like we do. “Pain is something we experience emotionally, it’s not just a prick on the finger,” says McCue.
